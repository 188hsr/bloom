Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bloom work?

Bloom asks you 15 questions about your current life, your 5-year vision, and your core values. Based on your answers, our AI generates a personalized list of 75 specific tasks to help you bridge the gap between where you are and where you want to be.

Why can I only see 10 tasks?

Free users can view their first 10 tasks. To unlock your complete personalized plan with all 75 tasks, you can purchase full access for a one-time payment of $4.99.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've previously purchased Bloom and need to restore access (for example, after reinstalling the app or switching devices), go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Can I start over with new answers?

Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Start Over." This will clear your current answers and task list so you can begin fresh. Note: this action cannot be undone. If you've purchased full access, you'll keep that access and can generate a new plan.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete My Account." This will permanently remove all your data from our systems. This action cannot be undone.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your responses and task list are stored securely and are only accessible to you. We do not sell or share your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Bloom a substitute for therapy or professional advice?

No. Bloom is a personal planning tool that provides general suggestions for goal-setting and personal growth. It is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, financial, or legal advice. If you need professional support, please consult a qualified professional.

The voice input isn't working. What should I do?

Make sure you've granted Bloom permission to access your microphone. You can check this in your iPhone's Settings → Bloom → Microphone. If you're still having trouble, you can always type your answers instead.
