Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Bloom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use Bloom, we collect:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique user identifier. We do not receive your email address unless you choose to share it.
  • Your Responses: The answers you provide to our 15 life planning questions are stored to generate and display your personalized task list.
  • Task Progress: We store which tasks you have marked as completed.
  • Purchase Information: We record whether you have purchased access to the full task list. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple; we do not receive or store your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Generate your personalized life planning task list
  • Save your progress so you can return to the app and continue
  • Restore your purchase if you reinstall the app or change devices

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

AI Processing

Your responses are sent to an AI service to generate your personalized task list. This processing occurs in real-time and your responses are not stored by the AI service beyond what is necessary to generate your plan.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain your data for as long as you have an active account. You can delete your data at any time through the app's Settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all the data we have stored about you (visible within the app)
  • Delete: Permanently delete all your data using the "Delete My Account" option in Settings
  • Start Over: Clear your responses and task list to begin fresh

Children's Privacy

Bloom is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy within the app. Your continued use of Bloom after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
